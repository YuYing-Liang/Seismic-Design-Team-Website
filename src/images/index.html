About Us

The University of Toronto Seismic Design Team (UTSD) aims to promote seismic engineering to the undergraduate engineering student body.

UTSD consists of both undergraduate and graduate students from both the engineering and architectural faculties collaborating to design a scale high rise structure out of balsa wood. The team will compete in the Annual Seismic Design Competition.
