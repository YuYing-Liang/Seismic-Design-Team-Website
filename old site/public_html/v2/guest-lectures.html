Guest Lectures

The Earthquake Engineering Research Institute (EERI) is responsible for hosting the Seismic Design Competition. As a soon to be official student chapter of the EERI, we have access to the amazing opportunity of having world-class industry members in the field of seismic design come speak here at the University of Toronto. As soon as affiliation is complete, we are seeking to have at least one guest-lecturer come this year to kick-off our first year as a design team!

Please check the Explore section below or send us an email on our Contact Us page to check when the guest lecture is taking place.
