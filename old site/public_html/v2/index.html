Seismic Kickoff: Sep 12 | BA3008 | 6:00 PM

Interested in knowing how to design tall buildings that can withstand large lateral forces from earthquake-induced ground motions? Join us as we kick off our second year as a team on September 12th, 2016 in BA3008 at 6 PM, where we will be sharing about our experience in finishing 4th last year in San Francisco, the lessons learned, our goals for this year’s design, and how our team operates. We’re looking for anyone who has a passion to learn more about structural engineering to join our dynamic team. Looking forward to meeting everyone!
