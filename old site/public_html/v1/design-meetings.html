Design Meetings

As a design team, it is imperative that we hold meetings to discuss, generate, critique, and develop ideas. We hold design meetings once a week for the first two months of first semester with the team executives, however, all members are encouraged to come out and voice their ideas, concerns, and questions. These meetings are where we propose the structural system for the year, and develop new ideas for that years design. We do our best to keep meetings short and efficient to keep everybody engaged!

Come November, the design has to be finished as the proposal is due for registration in the Annual Seismic Design Competition. These design meetings are replaced with two things. Firstly, construction time where teams of 3-4 students each meet once a week to work on the construction of the tower. Secondly, we hold construction team meetings to evaluate the efficiency of the construction process, address problems that come up, and track our progress.
