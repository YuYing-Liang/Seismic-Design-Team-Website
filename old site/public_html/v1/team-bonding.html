Team Bonding

As a design team, things can get busy very quickly so we like to ensure we find some down time just to hangout and socialize. Typically this involves simply hanging around after design meetings to catch up on things other than the project at hand. But, twice a year, we like to get together and have a movie-night. Our first movie on the menu is yet to be determined but will absolutely feature a seismic-related film!

We would love to have you join us for these fun times! Please check the Explore section below or send us an email on our Contact Us page to check when the movie nights are occurring!
